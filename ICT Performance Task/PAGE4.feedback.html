AESTHETICS

Its big and sparkling waves, availability of resorts, and warm people- these are factors that have made San Juan earn the title of surfing capital of the north. With its growing economic stability, the Nature is the main requirement for its sufficient progress. Every year, hundreds of tourists, many of them surfers, come to visit San Juan, which is a mere five-hour drive from Manila. As tourists visit this place, feedbacks are being encouraged for the management of San Juan, La Union, to know best development it can offer.
